# Lantern Engine Playtest 05

Date: 2026-03-10
Tester: Herra luutnantti
Build context: local v0.3.0 flashlight reveal proof

## Raw feedback

- Taskulamppu toimii muuten.
- Blink tapahtuu välittömästi.
- Valokeila ei hidasta sitä siten, että näkyisi välkkyen liikkumassa valokeilassa.

## Interpreted findings

### Confirmed success
- Flashlight itself works and is understood as a mechanic.

### Remaining issue
- The reveal/anti-blink effect is not yet communicating the intended fantasy strongly enough.
- Current implementation may reduce blink distance or reveal after the blink, but from the player's perspective the blink still resolves too instantly.
- The desired behavior is not just "weaker blink in stats" but a clearly visible slowed/hampered blink state while inside the cone.

## Design implication

The next fix should focus on **blink readability + interruption feel**, not on the flashlight base mechanic.

## Recommended next adjustment

Implement one clearly readable light-affected blink behavior:
- when Hollow is inside flashlight cone, blink gets a short visible degraded phase
- recommended MVP-safe version:
  - add brief pre-blink exposure/windup OR
  - replace instant blink with short flickering partial-travel dash in light
  - keep travel shorter than normal
  - keep the enemy dangerous, but readable

## Recommendation

Do not expand scope yet. Do a small follow-up pass focused only on making the light-vs-blink interaction visibly legible in motion.
