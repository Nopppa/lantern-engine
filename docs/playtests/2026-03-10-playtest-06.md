# Lantern Engine Playtest 06

Date: 2026-03-10
Tester: Herra luutnantti
Build context: local v0.3.0 flashlight reveal proof with blink readability follow-up

## Raw feedback

- Blink toimii nyt siten, että Hollow tärisee paikallaan hetken ja sitten teleporttaa seuraavaan paikkaan.
- Haluan, että blinkin alkukohta ja loppukohta tiedetään.
- Sen sijaan, että teleporttaa sinne kuten normaalisti, sen pitäisi edetä lineaarisesti alusta loppuun välkkyen.
- Liikkeen pitäisi olla selkeästi hitaampi kuin normaali blink.
- Mutta kuitenkin paljon nopeampi kuin normaali kävely.

## Interpreted findings

### Confirmed progress
- The pre-blink struggle/windup now reads to the player.

### Remaining issue
- The disrupted blink is still perceived as a teleport after the windup, not as a visibly compromised movement through light.
- The desired fantasy is not only “blink delayed” but “blink forced into a damaged, visible transit state.”

## Desired behavior clarified

When Hollow blinks inside flashlight influence:
- player should understand both the origin point and destination point
- movement should traverse visibly between them
- the transit should flicker / shimmer during travel
- travel should be faster than walking but much slower and more readable than a normal instant blink

## Recommendation

Next fix should replace the current post-windup teleport with a short visible disrupted-travel phase:
- compute blink start and target normally
- then animate a linear or near-linear flickering transit between them over a brief duration
- keep the shorter light-affected blink distance
- keep the enemy threatening, but no longer instantaneous inside the cone
